Brownie

These rich, fudgy brownies are topped are really appreciated everywhere.
This recipe in particular is one of the most popular dessert ever!

Brownie Ingredients

These are the ingredients you'll need to make the best brownie recipe ever:

  • butter
  • white sugar
  • eggs
  • vanilla extract
  • cocoa powder
  • all-purpose flour
  • salt
  • baking powder
Make the brownies
  1. Melt the butter on the stove
  2. remove from heat
  3. add the remaining wet ingredients.
  4. Beat in the dry ingredients
  5. Spread the batter in the pan and bake

How to Tell If Brownies Are Done

You'll know the brownies are done baking when the top is dry and the edges are pulling away from the pan.
Baked in an oven preheated to 350 degrees F, the brownies should be ready after about 25 to 30 minutes.
